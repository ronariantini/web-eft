  • Earth fault is considered very critical on board a ship.

    Any earth fault on a 440V system is regarded as a significant issue, and immediate action is required to identify the defective circuit. Any earth fault on a 220V or low voltage lighting circuit can be deemed significant, but does not necessarily require immediate attention. However, consideration must be given at the earliest opportunity.
